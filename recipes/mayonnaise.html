Mayonnaise

Description:

A rich and creamy condiment that is great for a sandwich or for dressing salad. This recipe will make about 16 servings.

Image and recipe source are from this site.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 teaspoon dry mustard powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 2 cups vegetable oil

Steps:

  1. Pour all contents (besides vegetable oil) into a food processor or blender.
  2. Set food processor to medium and gradually drizzle in the oil while it blends
  3. Transfer to a container with a lid and store in the refridgerator for up to 2 weeks.
